

## 🖤 1. Primary Theme (Base Identity)

Inspired by your logo:

* **Black** → `#000000`
* **White** → `#FFFFFF`
* **Dark Gray** → `#1A1A1A`
* **Light Gray** → `#F5F5F5`

💡 Why this works:
Black & white gives a **clean, professional, and timeless look**, often used in minimal branding to create strong contrast and readability ([Piktochart][1])

---

## ⚡ 2. Accent Color (Very Important)

To avoid boring UI, add **1 strong accent color**:

### Option A (Recommended 🔥 – Startup vibe)

* **Electric Yellow / Gold** → `#FFB800`

👉 Represents:

* Energy ⚡
* Innovation 🚀
* Youth (perfect for E-Cell)

---

### Option B (Tech + Premium)

* **Royal Blue** → `#2563EB`

👉 Represents:

* Trust
* Technology
* Professionalism

---

### Option C (Bold + Attention)

* **Crimson Red** → `#E63946`

👉 Represents:

* Action
* Urgency
* Leadership

---

# 🎯 Final Suggested Palette (Best Combo)

👉 I strongly recommend this:

```
Primary:   #000000 (Black)
Secondary: #FFFFFF (White)
Neutral:   #F5F5F5 / #1A1A1A (Grays)
Accent:    #FFB800 (Yellow/Gold)
```

---

# 🧩 How to Use It (UI Strategy)

### 🖥️ Background

* Main: White
* Sections: Light Gray

### 🧱 Navbar / Footer

* Black background
* White text

### 🔘 Buttons

* Primary CTA: Yellow (`#FFB800`)
* Hover: Slight darker (`#e6a700`)

### 📝 Text

* Headings: Black
* Body: Dark gray

### ✨ Highlights

* Use yellow for:

  * “Apply Now”
  * “Join E-Cell”
  * Event highlights

---

# 🎨 Visual Style Direction

You should aim for:

* Minimal (like Apple)
* Startup-style (like YC / Stripe)
* Clean typography + bold sections

💡 A monochrome base + accent color creates strong hierarchy and improves UX clarity ([Figma][2])

---

# 🚀 Bonus: Section-wise Idea

* **Hero Section** → Black background + yellow CTA
* **Events Page** → White cards + subtle gray
* **Team Page** → Clean white + hover effects
* **Sponsors Page** → Black + gold (premium feel)

---

# 💡 Final Thought

Your logo already screams:

> “Minimal + Elite + Startup Energy”

So don’t overcomplicate with too many colors.

👉 Stick to:
**Black + White + ONE Accent = 🔥 Perfect Brand**
